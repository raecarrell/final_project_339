All about Spanish Food

Spanish food is known for its rich flavor profiles that are created based upon the resources available within each region. Spanish cuisine is extremely diverse, there are significant regional impacts on the foods across Spain. A typical meal would start with appetizers, also known as "tapas". Tapas range from bread-centered dishes, seafood-centered dishes, and many other options as well. A typical Spanish meal would feature some main dish. This could be seafood, roasted meat, or even vegetables. An extremely popular Spanish dish is Paella, which is a rice dish that originally featured seafood. Modern-day Paella is more customizable. This would often be paired with sides like cheese, cold cuts, potatoes, salads, and more. A Spanish meal will end like any other cuisine. With dessert. Spanish dessert often depends on the occasion and season. Many Spanish desserts feature creamy flavor profiles. An extremely popular dessert is Crema Catalana, which could be thought of as a Spanish version of creme brulee.
